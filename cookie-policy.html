Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how The Scene ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and mobile application. By using our Services, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website or use our mobile application. They help us provide you with a better experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Understanding how you use our Services
  • Improving our Services based on your behavior
  • Providing you with relevant content and advertisements

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality, security, and account access Session - 1 year
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences Up to 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our Services Up to 2 years
Marketing Cookies Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track marketing effectiveness Up to 1 year

3. Specific Cookies We Use

  • Authentication cookies: To remember your login status and maintain your session
  • Preference cookies: To remember your language, location, and display preferences
  • Analytics cookies: We use services like Google Analytics to understand user behavior
  • Social media cookies: To enable social sharing and integration features

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. These may include:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
  • Payment processors
  • Advertising partners

5. Cookie Management

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Browser settings: Configure your browser to block or delete cookies
  • Device settings: Adjust privacy settings on your mobile device
  • Opt-out tools: Use available opt-out mechanisms for analytics and advertising

Please note that blocking certain cookies may impact the functionality of our Services.

6. Age Restrictions

As outlined in our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, our Services are only available to users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect or store cookies from individuals under 18 years of age.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated.

8. Canadian Privacy Laws

Our cookie practices comply with Canadian privacy laws, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in accordance with Canadian law.
