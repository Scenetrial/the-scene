Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2024

Welcome to The Scene. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our website and mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Website Information

  • Email addresses when you sign up for app launch notifications
  • Contact form submissions including name, email, and message content
  • Usage data such as pages visited and time spent on the website

1.2 Mobile App Information

  • Account information (email, name, profile details)
  • Location data (when permitted) to show nearby venues
  • Usage data including venue visits, preferences, and interactions
  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To send notifications about app launch and updates
  • To personalize venue and event recommendations
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To communicate with you about your account or inquiries
  • To ensure the security and proper functioning of our services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Partner venues (only aggregated, anonymized data)
  • Law enforcement when required by law

4. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Control location tracking and app permissions

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect or maintain information from persons under 18 years of age. If you are under 18, you are not permitted to use or access our Services at any time or in any manner. If we learn that personal information has been collected from persons under 18 years of age, we will take appropriate steps to delete this information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. This policy and our privacy practices are governed by the laws of Canada.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us at:

Email: info@thescenehub.com

Hours: Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST
